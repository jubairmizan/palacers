WHO WE ARE

COMPANY PROFILE

Palace Residence is the premium brand of Green Planet Lands Ltd which is one of the fastest growing real estate companies in Bangladesh. GPL is a sister concern of Blue Planet Group. Our company is built on core values of Integrity, Commitment & Passion. We have built robust businesses that excel at serving their customers and stakeholders through exceptional products and services in industries such as Spinning, Lingerie, Knitwear, Textile Composite, Hospitality & Tourism, Airline Cargo, Ayurveda and Investment Banking.

CEO’s MESSAGE

Shahjahan Shajedur, Deputy Managing Director & CEO

Assalamu Alaikum. It gives me immense pride to bring you Palace Residence a brand of Green Planet Lands Ltd which started with a dream to revolutionize the Real Estate sector in Bangladesh. It was founded recently to address the ever-increasing housing needs of the citizens in Dhaka.

We are successfully developing several integrated residential projects adjacent to the capital city and also taking up more projects in the vicinity of the capital.

The first project "Palace Residence 1" is adjacent to Rajuk Purbachal Sector- while the second project named "Palace Residence 2" is developing adjacent to DHAKA-SYLHET Highway near Purbachal. The third project which is upcoming is about 200 Bighas of land at Mowna, Gazipur near the capital to provide well planned “Duplex Villa” for people of Dhaka. The fourth upcoming project will be a sharing basis “5 Star Hotel Project" which is located at Srimongol near tea estates.

We look forward to become the leaders in this sector and be your reliable and trustworthy partner in real estate.
